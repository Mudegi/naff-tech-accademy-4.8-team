# Naf Academy User Guide

## Introduction

Naf Academy is a comprehensive educational platform designed to facilitate learning for students, teaching for educators, and monitoring for parents. This guide provides detailed instructions for School Students, School Teachers, and Parents on how to navigate and use the system effectively.

## For School Students

### Getting Started
1. **Login**: Use your school-provided credentials to log in at the main login page.
2. **Dashboard**: After logging in, you'll be redirected to your student dashboard.

### Dashboard Features
- **Overview**: View your recent activity, upcoming assignments, and performance summary.
- **My Resources**: Access study materials and resources provided by your school.
- **My Assignments**: View assigned tasks, submit completed work, and check grades.
- **My Performance**: Monitor your academic progress with detailed reports and analytics.
- **My Marks**: Review your grades and scores across subjects.
- **Career Guidance**: Explore course recommendations and career paths based on your performance.

### Accessing Videos
1. Navigate to "My Videos" from the sidebar.
2. Browse available videos filtered by subject, topic, term, or class.
3. Click on a video to watch it directly.
4. Use filters to narrow down content relevant to your studies.

### Managing Assignments
1. Go to "My Assignments" section.
2. View pending and completed assignments.
3. Click on an assignment to read instructions and submit your work.
4. Upload files or enter text responses as required.

### Performance Tracking
1. Access "My Performance" to view detailed analytics.
2. Review subject-wise performance, trends, and recommendations.
3. Use insights to improve your learning strategy.

### Communication
- **Chat**: Communicate with teachers and peers through the chat feature.
- **Notifications**: Stay updated with important announcements and reminders.

### Additional Features
- **Sample Videos**: If available, access free sample videos to explore content.
- **Learning Preferences**: Set your preferred subjects and learning style.
- **Subscription**: Manage your subscription for premium content (if applicable).

## For School Teachers

### Getting Started
1. **Login**: Use your teacher credentials to access the system.
2. **Dashboard**: Your dashboard provides an overview of your classes, assignments, and student performance.

### Managing Content
1. **My Videos**: Upload and manage educational videos.
   - Create new videos with titles, descriptions, and Google Drive links.
   - Organize by subject, topic, term, and class.
   - View student comments and respond to questions.
2. **Assignments**: Create and manage assignments.
   - Upload assignment files or create text-based tasks.
   - Assign to specific students or groups.
   - Set deadlines and grading criteria.

### Student Management
1. **Student Assignments**: Review submitted work and provide feedback.
2. **Assessment Management**: Create and manage assessments.
3. **Performance Monitoring**: Track student progress and identify areas for improvement.

### Communication
- **Messages**: Send and receive messages from parents and students.
- **Notifications**: Send announcements to your classes.

### Group Management
- Create and manage teacher groups for collaborative content creation.
- Assign resources to groups and track group performance.

### Additional Features
- **Student Marks**: View and update student grades.
- **Career Guidance**: Provide recommendations to students.
- **Subscription Management**: Monitor student subscriptions (if applicable).

## For Parents

### Getting Started
1. **Login**: Use your parent account credentials.
2. **Dashboard**: View an overview of your linked children's accounts and their performance.

### Monitoring Children
1. **Child Overview**: See a summary of each child's activity, assignments, and performance.
2. **View Videos**: Access educational videos available to your children.
   - Filter by child's grade level and subjects.
   - Watch videos to support your child's learning.
3. **Child Performance**: Review detailed performance reports for each child.
4. **Assignments**: Monitor assignment submissions and grades.

### Communication
- **Messages**: Communicate with teachers regarding your children's progress.
- **Notifications**: Receive updates on your children's activities.

### Managing Accounts
- **Profile**: Update your contact information and preferences.
- **Child Details**: View detailed information about each linked child.

### Additional Features
- **Subscription Plans**: If applicable, manage subscriptions for premium features.
- **Support**: Contact support for assistance with the platform.

## General Tips
- **Security**: Always log out after use, especially on shared devices.
- **Browser Compatibility**: Use modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, or Edge for best experience.
- **Mobile Access**: The platform is responsive and works on mobile devices.
- **Support**: Contact your school administrator or Naf Academy support for technical issues.

## Troubleshooting
- **Login Issues**: Ensure correct credentials and check for caps lock.
- **Video Loading**: Check internet connection and try refreshing the page.
- **Assignment Submission**: Ensure file formats are supported and within size limits.
- **Performance Reports**: If data seems incorrect, contact your teacher or administrator.

This guide covers the main features of Naf Academy. For more detailed information or updates, refer to in-app help or contact support.
